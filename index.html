About


Artilicious is an art blogging page maintained by Fiola Mathias to showcase her artworks.It is used as a blog page to document artworks made using various mediums and to show the medium that are being explored currently.




It is used to show paintings including mixed media arts which include poster, gouache and watercolor paintings as well as oil pastel works and portraits.
